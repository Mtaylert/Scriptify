Job Description:
The Lead ML Ops Engineer at Fidelity Labs is a pivotal role for the continued success and growth of the Catchlight startup. Catchlight is a new software solution that helps financial advisors prioritize and optimize their leads and prospects. Catchlight launched in January 2022 and is now available to financial advisors across the United States. Visit https://catchlight.ai(opens in a new tab) for more information.

In this role you will be responsible for building and maintaining the next generation ML platform and infrastructure. The Lead ML Ops Engineer will work closely with technical leads, infrastructure engineers and data scientists. They will establish best practices and lead initiatives to simplify the development and deployment of ML models at scale and bring game-changing ideas to market.

The Expertise and Skills You Bring

Extensive software engineering experience including distributed systems and data infrastructure

Strong knowledge of Python

Experience with ML infrastructure and ML Ops:

Building tooling and automation to support the entire ML engineering lifecycle from experimentation to production operations

AWS Sagemaker and Airflow

Kubernetes, Terraform and ML CI/CD workflows

Strong understanding of data science, core ML concepts and approaches

Passion for testing as a core part of the development process

You thrive in fast-paced, ambiguous environments with high risks and high rewards
